Child of mass destruction

On January 20th a tragedy struck. Our once trusted and highly regarded Powerpuff girl "Bubbles" went rogue in the city of Townsville. It's stated from a trusted insider that bullying is what triggered the super hero into assaulting the city. After a proper degrading from her sisters after not being able to handle a higher level battle simulation, bubbles decided to show them who's "hardcore". She began by slamming her sister Buttercup into the ground during a seemingly innocent game of tag. While her sisters looked in shock at what they just witnessed thier baby sister do, Bubbles rushed to the hotline to rudely answer the major. "I had just asked her to clear up the traffic jam...I had no idea this would be the result" Stated the major in a recent interview. Instead of directing traffic accordingly, she instead terrorize our most injured victim, Talking dog.Bubbles was caught on film viciously tearing open Talking Dog's car and ripping him from the driver's seat. She then began beating the Dog sensly before she threw him to the ground. "I begged for mercy but she didn't care. The last thing I remember is her telling me she's 'hardcore'. Next thing I know I'm in the ICU!" Talking Dog said through tears. When asked why the dog had held up traffic for an hour, he responded "I don't know how to drive" Her sisters later appeared at the scene looking shocked but not intervening. Talking Dog sustained life changing injuries and a multitude of hospital bills and isn't the only victim in this debacle. Multiple citizens have come forward with their stories and injuries for petty crimes ranging from littering to stepping on protected grass. Bubbles rage has changed the dynamic of the city for the worse and the people want answers. This tragedy has opened a full investigation into the powerpuff girls and the professor. Will Townsville ever be safe again? Or will the citizens be forever subjected to the violent tantrums of our so-called "heros".
